About:

My name is Zachariah (Zach) Zobair and I am an incoming mathematics Ph.D. student at Duke University. Previously, I was at Boston University, where I completed my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in pure mathematics. My Master's thesis advisor was Professor Alexander Bertoloni Meli.

I broadly like stuff that lies in the world of algebraic number theory and algebraic geometry. More specifically, I recently have been enjoying studying things relating to the Langlands program and automorphic representations.
